BRASS ORGANUTHING

            In the heart of the jungle, back in '85, 
            Lived an orangutan with the coolest vibe.
            He wore baggy pants and a jacket so bright,
            Under neon lights, he was quite the sight.

            Bananas? He'd scoff, with a look so sour,
            "Not for me," he'd say, "not now, not this hour."
            He craved the beats, the rhythm, and flow,
            Hip-hop was his love, this you must know.
